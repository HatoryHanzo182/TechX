Create new product

Here you can create a product for your website. Some of the vehicle models are already saved, so you do not have to manually fill in all the characteristics. After creating the product, go to the "All Products" tab.

Edit product

Here you can edit the content of your products. Attention, only those products that are not yet listed on the site are edited.

Display and manage all products

All your products will be displayed here. After adding products, you can display it on the site, or leave the best time. If the product is no longer needed, the delete function is available.
